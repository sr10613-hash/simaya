Panda



A panda is a mammal that grows up to 1.2–1.8 meters and weighs around 90–136 kg. Pandas are deadly because of their extreme strength and their sharp teeth/claws.

There are only of 2 panda species, these are both of them and where you can find them:

  • The Giant Panda - Most Common - The temperate mountain forests of South-central China
  • The Red Panda - Less Common - Temperate bamboo forests in the Eastern Himalayas and southwestern China, specifically within Nepal, India, Bhutan, Myanmar, and China
