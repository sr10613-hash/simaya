GOLDEN POISON FROG

A golden poison frog is a amphibian that grows up to 5-6cm and weighs around 30 grams kg. Golden poison frogs are deadly because their venom is extremely poisonous, as a single specimen measuring two inches has enough venom to kill ten grown men. Even though the golden poison frog is highly poisonous, they are quite endangered.

The golden poison frog is one of many frog species, some other deadly frog species are:

  • Black-legged Poison Dart Frog
  • Kokoe Poison Frog
  • Phantasmal Poison Frog
  • Blue Poison Dart Frog
